Behavior Grid

lengths of the sides of a triangle

BEHAVIOR                INPUT EXAMPLE (side lengths)           OUTPUT EXAMPLE
is it equilateral?     (60, 60, 60)                            true
is it isosceles?       (60, 60, 40)                            true
is it scalene?         (60, 70, 80)                            true
is it a triangle?      (70, 200, 70)                           false

1) equilateral: all three sides are equal
2) isosceles: 2 sides of equal length
3) scalene: all its sides of different length
4) NOT a triangle
